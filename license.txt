Twitched is a Roku (media player) app for connecting to Twitch (live streaming service)
and watching live streams. The Twitched website/API serves as a proxy/cache for all request to
Twitch.

Twitched is not affiliated with Twitch Interactive, Inc. or Roku, Inc.
